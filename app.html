Radio-Frequency Interference (RFI) Filter

This application filters Radio Frequency Interference (RFI) from .dada acquisition files recorded by radio telescopes. The data are complex numbers, separated into real and imaginary parts before applying two simultaneous filters:

  • Standard Deviation (STD): \(\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N(x_i - \mu)}\)
  • Median Absolute Deviation (MAD): \(MAD = \mathrm{median}(|X_i - \tilde{X}|)\)

The goal is to detect and remove outliers, reconstruct clean complex signals, and export them into a new .dada file.
