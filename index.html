Welcome to the home page of my website.

I am a PhD student at INSA Rennes, with a joint appointment at the IETR laboratory. My current research efforts concentrate on rapid prototyping for HPC systems deploying astronomical applications using dataflow optimization. This work is supported by the ANR project Dark Era, which aims to build SimSDP, an exascale simulation tool designed for early analysis of the SKA radio telescope. The SKA project, a major advancement in radio astronomy, consists of antennas and dishes located in South Africa and Australia. It converts voltages into visibility through the CSP pipeline processor and transforms these visibilities into output images using SDP processors, processing several terabytes per second. This poses a significant resource allocation challenge, which I am addressing.

My research benefits from collaborations and knowledge exchanges through the Rising Stars initiative.
