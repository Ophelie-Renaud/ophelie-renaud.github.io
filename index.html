Welcome

I am a post-doctoral researcher at ENS Rennes, with a joint appointment at SATIE, ENS Paris Saclay and IRISA, MAGELLAN team. I obtained my PhD in Signal, Image, Vision at INSA Rennes, with a joint appointment at the IETR laboratory in 2024.

My current research efforts concentrate on rapid prototyping for HPC systems deploying astronomical applications using dataflow optimization. My work is supported by the ANR project Dark Era, which aims to build SimSDP, an exascale simulation tool designed for early analysis of the SKA radio telescope.

My research benefits from collaborations and knowledge exchanges through the Rising Stars initiative.
